Questions

Restaurant Owners Ask Us.

Will the AI sound like a robot to my guests?
No. We train it on your restaurant's own voice and menu, so replies read like a friendly host wrote them. You approve the tone before anything ever goes live.
Do I need new software or a tech person?
No. It works with your existing phone number and booking tools. There is nothing for your staff to learn and no new hardware to buy.
What happens with a bad review?
Negative reviews are flagged for you and never posted automatically. You stay in full control of how you respond to anything sensitive.
How long until it is live?
Usually about a week from our first call. We build a working setup, you test it, and we adjust it before it ever touches a real guest.
How much does it cost?
It depends on what you automate. The 15-minute discovery call is free, and we scope a plan around your covers and call volume with clear, flat pricing.
