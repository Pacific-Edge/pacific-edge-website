Questions

Clinic Owners Ask Us.

Is patient information kept private?
Privacy comes first. The AI handles scheduling and reminders without exposing sensitive health details, and you stay in control of anything that needs a human. We build to respect Canadian privacy expectations.
Will it sound robotic to my patients?
No. We train it on your clinic's tone so it feels calm and professional, like your own front desk. You approve the voice before it speaks to a patient.
Does it work with our practice management software?
Yes. It works alongside the phone number and scheduling tools your office already uses, so bookings and recalls land where your team already works.
How long until it is live?
Usually about a week from our first call. We set it up, your team tests it, and we fine-tune it before it ever speaks to a patient.
How much does it cost?
It depends on what you automate. The 15-minute discovery call is free, and we scope a flat-priced plan around your call volume and chair count.
